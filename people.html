Notable People from Boston

John Adams

Second President of the United States and a key figure in the American Revolution.

Paul Revere

Silversmith and patriot known for his midnight ride during the American Revolution.

Benjamin Franklin

Founding Father, inventor, and diplomat who helped secure French support during the American Revolution.

Samuel Adams

Political philosopher and one of the Founding Fathers of the United States, known for his role in the Boston Tea Party.

Robert Frost

Renowned poet who won four Pulitzer Prizes for his work.

George Washington

First President of the United States and leader during the American Revolutionary War.

Louisa May Alcott

Author best known for her novel "Little Women."

Henry James

Author and critic known for works such as "The Turn of the Screw" and "The Portrait of a Lady."

Jack Welch

Former CEO of General Electric, known for his management practices and leadership style.

Matt Damon

Actor and screenwriter, known for his roles in "Good Will Hunting" and the "Jason Bourne" series.

Ben Affleck

Actor, director, and screenwriter, known for "Argo" and "Gone Girl."

Mark Wahlberg

Actor and producer, known for "The Departed" and "Ted."

Barbara Walters

Television personality and journalist known for her work on "The View" and "20/20."

John Hancock

Signatory of the Declaration of Independence and prominent patriot during the American Revolution.

Michael Bloomberg

Business magnate, philanthropist, and former mayor of New York City.

Robert F. Kennedy

Senator and Attorney General, known for his advocacy for civil rights and social justice.

Neal Stephenson

Science fiction author known for "Snow Crash" and "Cryptonomicon."

Conan O'Brien

Television host and comedian, known for "Late Night with Conan O'Brien" and "Conan."

Edgar Allan Poe

Writer known for his macabre and gothic tales such as "The Raven" and "The Tell-Tale Heart."

Judy Boston

Historical figure known for her contributions to early Boston education and social reform.

Christina Ricci

Actress known for her roles in "The Addams Family" and "Sleepy Hollow."

William Lloyd Garrison

Famous abolitionist and journalist, known for his anti-slavery newspaper, "The Liberator."
